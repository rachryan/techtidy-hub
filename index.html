What I do — and what I don’t

I want you to know exactly where I’m the right fit — and when you might need someone else.

  • Organizing files, email, and cloud storage so you can actually find things.
  • Setting up password managers, safer logins, and basic security hygiene.
  • Making Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace feel clear, not chaotic.
  • Teaching AI tools in plain language, with real-life workflows.

I’m not the right fit for…

  • Repairing broken laptops, phones, or hardware.

  • 24/7 emergency support or “always on” monitoring.
